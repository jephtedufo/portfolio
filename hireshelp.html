Hireshelp

Software Developer and Designer at Hireshelp

August, 2022 December, 2024

I started Hireshelp in August 2022 as a software developer, where I built the company’s website from the ground up. Before that, I created their logo and brand identity, setting up the look and feel of the brand. Once the branding was done, I moved on to marketing, designing social media posts, banners, and ads using Canva. This was actually the beginning of my graphic design journey, where I first started learning how to create designs on different softwares.

I also worked with the lead software engineer to help develop the Hireshelp app, which was meant to help unemployed people find jobs, help freelancers get more clients, and get businesses easier lands on talented hires. Unfortunately, the project stopped halfway and never launched, but it was still a great learning experience. During my time at Hireshelp, I learned a lot about Canva, FlutterFlow, app design, and how to publish apps on the Play Store. Even though the project didn’t go as planned, it helped me develop valuable skills in design, software development, and branding—skills I still use today.
